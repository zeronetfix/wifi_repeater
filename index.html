Zeronetfix Wifi Repeater

Choose Firmware

You Can Flash Zeronetfix Wifi Repeater Only On Esp8266 With This

Note
  • Zeronetfix Wifi Repeater support esp8266 only
  • It does not support esp32 or others
How To Flash Zeronetfix Wifi Repeater Firmware to ESP8266-based Board
  • Select Firmware
  • Connect your ESP8266 board via USB.
  • Click "Connect" and choose the serial port of your ESP.
  • On Device Dashboard, click "Install".
How To Open Web-Based Serial Monitor
  • Select Firmware
  • Connect your ESP8266 board via USB.
  • Click "Connect" and choose the serial port of your ESP.
  • On Device Dashboard, click "Logs and Console".
